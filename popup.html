Fill-it

...I feel it good...

Fill-it is Google Chrome extension design to fill HTML form with fake values in order to saving time when it comes to fill forms. This extension is totally customisable by changing JSON parameters.

Auto fill all : CTRL + SHIFT + A

Auto fill one : CTRL + DOUBLE CLICK

Auto fill form : CTRL + SHIFT + F

You can define an area into "form" with "data-filler" attribut (Instance : Ajax forms without "form" tag)
